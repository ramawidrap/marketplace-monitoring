- No prices were fabricated or inferred from search-snippet summaries alone — per the anti-fabrication rule, only figures confirmed by directly fetching a page (Tokopedia only, this run) are reported in the Listings/Summary tables.

**Bottom line:** Tokopedia discovery + verification both worked this run and turned up real listings, all above threshold.
